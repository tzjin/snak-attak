About
SnikSnak is here for those who care about food. We are what we eat, so let's fill our tummies with what's good. Discover the best food items of the day, as recommended by others. Eat happy, be happy.

Available on the web, iOS, and Android.
Find Food Faster
Quickly choose what you want, where you want, when you want. Easily select filters for meal, dining hall, and allergens/diets to see what's available today!
See what's Good
Get the inside scoop from others who have eaten already, and make an educated decision on what to eat. Afterwards, give back by voting on the food you did and did not like!
